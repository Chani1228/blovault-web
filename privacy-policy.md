BLO VAULT ("we", "us") operates the BLO VAULT mobile application.

## 1. DATA WE COLLECT

BLO VAULT collects financial account data via Plaid with your explicit consent. All transaction data is stored exclusively on your device in an AES-256 encrypted database. We do not store your financial data on our servers.

## 2. ON-DEVICE AI

All AI inference runs entirely on your device using Gemma 3 (Cactus SDK). Your financial data is never sent to AI servers.

## 3. SUPABASE (BACKEND)

We use Supabase solely as a Plaid proxy. Only your encrypted Plaid access token is stored in Supabase. Raw transaction data never reaches our servers.

## 4. CCPA RIGHTS (California Residents)

You have the right to: (a) know what personal data we collect; (b) delete your personal data; (c) opt out of sale of personal data (we do not sell data); (d) non-discrimination.

## 5. DATA DELETION

Use **Panic Delete** in Settings to permanently wipe all data from your device and request deletion of your Supabase account data.
